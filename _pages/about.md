I am an **engineer-researcher** interested in **cyber-physical systems (CPS)** combined with **AI**, especially in the context of **embedded systems**. I am fascinated by how software and hardware interact to create intelligent systems that can sense, decide, and respond in the real world. I love coding, particularly in **C** and **C++**, and I am also comfortable with **Python, Rust, Java, and JavaScript**.

Beyond research, I love **photography**, especially capturing people, animals, and landscapes. I also enjoy **road trips** that lead to unexpected moments, like deer or raccoons casually blocking the road in Shenandoah National Park.
